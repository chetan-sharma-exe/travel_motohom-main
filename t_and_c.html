Cancellation Policy

-Any cancellations initiated by the customer will have a 10% charge at all times.

-Any cancellations initiated by the customer within the 48hrs of the scheduled time will be 100% chargeable, 12 days prior the trip then 20% chargeable, 7 days prior the trip will be 30% chargeable, 4 days prior the trip will be 50% chargeable.

-Any cancellations initiated by Motohom for any operational reasons before the start of the trip are refunded 100% at all times. Refunds are done within 7 working days.

-You may cancel the trip only if the caravan is not clean or does not meet hygiene standards, but the cancellation must be made within 15 minutes after the caravan arrives at your location. No cancellations will be accepted after this period.

-In case of caravan breakdown, before arrival at destination will be compensated with the time delayed. Breakdown after completing 50% of the trip, will be only compensated with making arrangements to the drop point as booked, also in case of breakdown, trip will be charged till breakdown only if the caravan is not repairable.

-In the event of a breakdown, customers are not allowed to leave their luggage behind. We will not transport luggage separately if the customer is not present in the caravan at any location. This is to avoid any false allegations regarding the safety and handling of personal belongings.

-As with any machine, there is a possibility of breakdowns. While we ensure that the caravan is fully maintained and checked before dispatch, we cannot guarantee that issues will not occur during your trip. Please understand that breakdowns are sometimes inevitable.

-If the caravan breaks down during your trip, we will arrange alternate transportation (e.g., a vehicle to drop you at your location, a nearby railway station, bus stop, or airport). The vehicle provided will be comfortable for you and will be chosen to fit within reasonable budget constraints, considering what is financially viable for both parties.
