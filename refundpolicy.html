Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to www.motohom.co.in

We at MOTOHOM PRIVATE LIMITED (in short 'MOTOHOM', 'we', 'us', 'our') are committed to our refund and cancellation provisions under applicable laws.

For internet-based subscription / booking services, You are free to cancel the booking /service at any time. MOTOHOM shall refund any unexpended advance fees paid by You at the time of cancellation of the booking / service. In case You wish to seek a refund of the advance paid by You because You do not agree with a modification to the Agreement, please write to grievance@motohom.co.in. We will process refund of the unexpended amount.

Subjected to terms under clause ‘Use of Services’ of this Agreement for all home-based Services, MOTOHOM shall provide refund of any unexpended advance fees paid by You if:

1. MOTOHOM is not able to deliver the Services and/or Products for any reason. In case You wish to seek a refund of the advance paid by You because You believe that MOTOHOM Service is not as per the specification provided on the Application, please write to support@motohom.co.in. MOTOHOM will process refund of the unexpended amount provided internal investigation confirms the claim made by you.

2. If the user cancels a booking more than two hours before scheduled appointment time an automated refund will be issued after deducting transaction fee, if any

3. If user cancels a booking within two hours of scheduled appointment time 50% automated refund will be done.

4. In case of no show by the user during appointed / booked time no refund will be generated.

5. If booking is taken within two hours of scheduled time and the user does not show up, full automated refund will be done.

6. Otherwise, if the user does not show up during appointed time no refund will be issued.

7. If the trip does not happen due to any special circumstance including technical issues, an option to request refund will be available to the user for a period of 7 days but the issuance of full or part refund will be on the sole discretion of MOTOHOM.

8. If booking is cancelled due to technical issues by MOTOHOM than full refund will be made.

9. All refunds will be subject to a transaction charge that will be deducted while making a refund.

In case You wish to seek a refund of the advance paid by You, please write to grievance@motohom.co.in. We will process refund of the unexpended amount at the discretion of MOTOHOM on case-to-case basis
